# Payment Gateway Risk Notes

Independent-store policy pages should avoid absolute promises around approval, refunds, chargeback outcomes, customs handling, or anonymous settlement.

Common evidence requested during PayPal or Stripe review:

- refund policy and return window
- shipping policy and fulfillment timelines
- product page screenshots
- checkout screenshots
- supplier and inventory explanation
- customer-support records
- order and tracking samples

High-risk wording patterns:

- guaranteed approval
- risk-free refund
- anonymous payment
- instant customs clearance
- no chargeback risk

Safer framing should describe documented operations, evidence availability, dispute process, and realistic customer-support windows.
