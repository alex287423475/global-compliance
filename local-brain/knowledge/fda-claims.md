# FDA / Medical Claim Red Lines

For pet devices, wellness products, cosmetics, supplements, and smart hardware, content must avoid disease-treatment or medical-effect language unless the operator has appropriate regulatory evidence.

High-risk terms:

- cure
- treat
- heal
- medical grade
- clinically proven
- disease prevention
- separation anxiety cure

Safer alternatives:

- support routine
- help organize usage
- designed for monitoring
- evidence-backed setup guidance
- documented specification

The page should separate SEO demand capture from regulatory claims. Sensitive phrases may appear in internal redline lists, but should not appear in public titles, checkout promises, policy claims, or advertising claims.
